Trying out a
new restaurant

At QD's, the atmosphere exudes relaxation and warmth, offering a perfect escape from the bustling city. My recent visit was defined by the tantalizing Lotus Biscoff waffle and ice cream, a highlight showcased on their webpage.
The chill vibe of QD's made it an ideal spot to unwind. The Lotus Biscoff waffle, crispy yet fluffy, paired harmoniously with the creamy ice cream, delivering a burst of caramelized flavors with every bite.
Beyond the dessert, QD's culinary offerings impressed with their attention to detail and diverse menu. It provided a haven where friends gathered and individuals found solace in culinary delights.
As I left QD's, satisfied and rejuvenated, the experience lingered, a testament to its commitment to flavor and ambiance. For those seeking a culinary adventure intertwined with relaxation, QD's is a must-visit destination.
